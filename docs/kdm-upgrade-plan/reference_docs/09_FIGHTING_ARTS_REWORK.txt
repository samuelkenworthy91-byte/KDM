FIGHTING ARTS REWORK

Goal:
Fighting arts should match the current gear-card combat system. Avoid flat '+1 Strength' style effects unless rare.

Current combat systems to hook into:
- card played
- card type: attack, skill, support, reaction, counter
- energy cost
- damage
- block
- break damage
- weak point opened/broken
- harvest quality
- statuses: bleed, burn, poison, doom, vulnerable, staggered, guarded, snared, shock, blind, prepared, marked, exposed
- colour affinity on card/source gear
- weaponStrategyId if available
- gearInstanceId and card-copy practice progress
- Panic gained/removed
- survivor death
- retreat
- combat start/end

Add hook engine before complex art data:
Suggested hooks:
- onCombatStart
- onCardCost
- onCardPlayed
- onDamageCalculated
- onBlockGained
- onDamagePrevented
- onStatusApplied
- onWeakPointOpened
- onWeakPointBroken
- onHarvestRolled
- onPanicGained
- onPanicRemoved
- onSurvivorDeath
- onCombatEnd
- onHuntReturn
- onRetreat

New fighting art batch should use current systems. Example style:
- Blue Knuckle Doctrine: first time damage is fully prevented with Block/Guarded, counter damage equals Blue affinity count.
- Second Knife Truth: if second attack comes from same gear instance, +1 break and +1 practice progress.
- Harvest Without Mercy: on Precise/Harvest weak-point break, improve harvest quality by one step and gain Panic 1.
- Purple Gut Instinct: once per hunt, convert a failed harvest/retreat/weak-point test by 2 or less into Messy success by spending 1 Survival.

Do not implement all fighting arts as hard-coded if/else blocks in App.jsx.
Use data-driven trigger/effect definitions as far as possible.
