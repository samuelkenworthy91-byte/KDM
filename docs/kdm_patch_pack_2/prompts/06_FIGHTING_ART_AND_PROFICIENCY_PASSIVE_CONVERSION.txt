Convert fighting arts and weapon proficiency from deck-added cards into passives.

Goal:
Reduce deck bloat and make survivor growth operate as visible passive/active abilities rather than extra cards.

Design:
- Gear provides most deck cards.
- Panic/curse/injury/story/nemesis champion cards may still enter decks.
- Fighting arts become passive effects or active buttons.
- Weapon proficiency becomes passive effects by level.
- Survivors benefit from every proficiency passive up to their current level.

Strict rules:
- Do not remove gear cards.
- Do not remove Panic cards.
- Do not remove nemesis champion cards.
- Preserve existing survivors with old fighting art/proficiency cards by migration.
- Do not break combat hooks.
- Do not broadly rewrite combatLogic.

Required behaviour:

1. Fighting arts:
- Stop adding ordinary fighting art cards to run decks.
- Convert current fighting arts to passive definitions.
- Some fighting arts may define active buttons.
- Active fighting art buttons should appear near the survival action bar or hunter action bar.
- Active buttons need usage limits: once per fight, once per round, or as defined.

2. Weapon proficiency:
- Stop adding proficiency reward cards to run decks.
- Proficiency level gives passive effects.
- Level 3 includes levels 1 and 2.
- Existing weapon proficiency combat bonuses should still work.
- Preview/passive registry should show which bonuses are active.

3. Migration:
- If old survivors have fighting art/proficiency cards in personalDeckAdditions solely because of old systems, remove or convert them safely.
- Do not remove real personal story/nemesis/Panic cards.

4. UI:
- Survivor details and hunter panel/passive registry should show passive fighting arts and proficiency effects.
- If a fighting art has an active button, show it with clear remaining uses.

5. Tests:
- building a run deck no longer adds fighting art/proficiency cards.
- existing gear still adds gear cards.
- Panic/story/nemesis cards still can appear.
- fighting art passive still affects combat.
- active fighting art button can be used and respects usage limit.
- proficiency level 2 includes level 1 passive.
- old save migration removes obsolete deck cards safely.
- passive registry exposes fighting art and proficiency passives.

Validation:

npm run test
