Rework nemesis encounters into deadly champion duels with champion reward cards.

Goal:
Nemesis encounters should be one-on-one, deadly, and rewarding if won.

Design:
- Nemesis appears.
- Player chooses one champion.
- Champion fights alone.
- Nemesis is far more dangerous than normal quarry fights.
- Victory gives settlement resources and a unique champion card to the winner.
- Failure kills/scars/punishes the champion heavily.

Required behaviour:

1. Nemesis setup:
- Keep fight one-on-one.
- Do not let full party fight unless a future special rule says so.
- UI should clearly say this is a Champion Duel.

2. Deadlier nemesis:
- Increase nemesis HP/damage/status pressure carefully.
- Make intent/deck behaviour more threatening.
- Add tests around target damage/lethality.
- Do not make it impossible at early required points.

3. Victory rewards:
- Gain a few basic resources.
- Gain rare/random monster resources from a random unlocked quarry.
- Champion gains one unique nemesis champion card.
- Reward card goes into that survivor's personal deck/run deck.
- Champion card should be very strong and usually Exhaust.

4. Champion cards:
Create original cards, one per nemesis.
Example design:
- Butcher-style card: low cost, huge damage, Exhaust, maybe applies Bleed/Stagger/Vulnerable.
- Other nemeses get equally distinctive powerful cards.

Do not copy exact KDM names or text like "Hack City". Use original names.

5. UI:
- Nemesis result screen shows:
  - champion
  - resources won
  - champion card gained
  - what the card does
- Survivor detail shows the champion card.

6. Tests:
- nemesis fight is one champion only.
- victory grants resources from unlocked quarry pool.
- victory grants champion card to the correct survivor only.
- champion card appears in run deck.
- champion card exhausts/limits correctly.
- defeat kills/scars/punishes champion.
- reward cannot be duplicated.
