Implement campaign principle effects.

Goal:
Wire real gameplay effects for Death, New Life, and Society principles.

Dependencies:
Run after 03_CAMPAIGN_PRINCIPLES_ENGINE.

Principle effects:

1. Death Principle
Triggered after first survivor death if death principle not chosen.

Graves:
- Each survivor death gives +1 Memory.
- Applies to the first death that triggered the choice.
- Applies automatically to future deaths.
- Reward must be logged in memoryHistory/settlementHistory.

Cannibalism:
- Each survivor death gives +1 random basic resource.
- Applies to the first death that triggered the choice.
- Applies automatically to future deaths.
- Reward must be logged in settlementHistory.
- Use only basic resources.

2. New Life Principle
Triggered after first newborn/first child if newLife principle not chosen.

Protect the Young:
- +10 percentage points intimacy success chance.
- -10 percentage points intimacy tragedy chance.
- All newborns gain +5 max HP.
- Applies to the newborn that triggered the choice.

Survival of the Fittest:
- +20 percentage points intimacy tragedy chance.
- All newborns gain a permanent personal +2 damage modifier/effect.
- All newborns gain +1 max Survival.
- Applies to the newborn that triggered the choice.
- The +2 damage should affect that survivor's own attacks, not the whole party.

3. Society Principle
Triggered at Lantern Year 5 if society principle not chosen.

Work Together:
- Once per Lantern Year, any one thing that costs exactly 1 Memory costs 0 instead.
- Applies to innovation attempts, Rite of Forgetting, Memory Training, Weapon Drills, Quiet Night, and future 1-Memory actions.
- UI/preview must show original cost, Work Together discount, final cost, and whether it is already used this Lantern Year.
- Store yearly use safely in settlement.principleUses.

Embrace the Dark:
- Affects rest stops only.
- Negative rest outcomes -10 percentage points.
- Neutral rest outcomes -10 percentage points.
- Positive rest outcomes +20 percentage points.
- Rest stop odds must still total 100%.
- UI must show shifted odds or at least say Embrace the Dark modified this rest stop.

Strict rules:
- Do not allow principle rewards to be claimed twice.
- Do not require repeated choices after the principle is locked.
- Do not leave the old repeated grave legacy reward system active.
- Do not break awardHuntReturnMemories for living survivors.
- Do not create negative probabilities.

Deletion/replacement required:
- Replace old resolveDeathMemoryChoice bury/recover-resource behaviour.
- Replace old GraveLegacyScreen repeated choice assumptions.
- Rewrite memoryEconomy tests that say burial gives no memory.
- Prevent duplicate graveHistory entries where possible.
