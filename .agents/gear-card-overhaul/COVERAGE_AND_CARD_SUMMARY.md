## Design approach

Every row keeps the original equipment name as an audit anchor and gives our original gear name, a method-to-make column, up to four concrete card texts, and passive effects. The cards are designed around the gear's inferred weapon type and keywords so the later Codespace overhaul can convert the table into game data.

The pack deliberately avoids copying exact KDM rules text. It uses the uploaded reference as an audit list and converts roles into original deckbuilder mechanics.
