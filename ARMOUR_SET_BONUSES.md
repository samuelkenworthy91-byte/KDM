# Armour Set Bonuses
## Pale Hunter Armour
- Source/crafting location: Catarium
- Pieces (5): Pale Hunter Crown, Pale Hunter Coat, Pale Hunter Gloves, Pale Hunter Skirt, Pale Hunter Treads
- 2-piece minor: +1 block the first time you play an attack each round.
- 4-piece major: After you deal damage once per round, apply Marked 1.
- Complete huge: At the start of each survivor turn, choose +1 damage this turn or Dodge 1.
- Complete card: **Pale Hunter — Lantern Pounce** — Deal 4 damage. Apply Marked 2. If you already have Dodge, draw 1 then discard 1.

## Dusk Hunter Armour
- Source/crafting location: Dark Catarium
- Pieces (5): Shadow Fur Crown, Shadow Fur Coat, Shadow Fur Gloves, Shadow Fur Kilt, Shadow Fur Treads
- 2-piece minor: The first incoming attack each round loses 1 damage.
- 4-piece major: When you play a Counter, gain Dodge 1 after it resolves.
- Complete huge: Once per battle, ignore the first Bleed, Poison, Burn or Doom you would gain.
- Complete card: **Dusk Hunter — Vanish Under Fur** — Cancel 2 incoming damage. Move 1 space. Your next attack this round gains +1 damage.

## Cloth Armour
- Source/crafting location: Cotton Mill
- Pieces (5): Cloth Turban, Cloth Robes, Cloth Bracers, Cloth Pants, Cloth Galoshes
- 2-piece minor: When you draw a support card, gain Guarded 1.
- 4-piece major: Your first support card each round costs 1 less, minimum 0.
- Complete huge: When you would gain Panic, reduce it by 1 and give an adjacent ally Guarded 1.
- Complete card: **Cloth Armour — Emergency Binding** — Heal 1 wound from yourself or an adjacent ally. Then gain Guarded 2.

## Cycloid Scale Armour
- Source/crafting location: Sky Reef Sanctuary
- Pieces (5): Cycloid Scale Hood, Cycloid Scale Jacket, Cycloid Scale Sleeves, Cycloid Scale Skirt, Cycloid Scale Shoes
- 2-piece minor: After you move 2 or more spaces, gain +1 block until the next quarry action.
- 4-piece major: The first ranged or reach attack you play each round gains +1 damage.
- Complete huge: Once per round after moving, you may cleanse Snared 1 or Exposed 1 from yourself.
- Complete card: **Cycloid Scale — Reef Glide** — Move up to 3 spaces. Gain Dodge 1. Your next attack this turn applies Exposed 1.

## Ember Drake Armour

- Pieces (5): Ember Drake Skull Crown, Ember Drake Mantle, Ember Drake Gloves, Ember Drake Belt, Ember Drake Treads
- 2-piece minor: When you apply Burn, increase that Burn by 1 once per round.
- 4-piece major: At the end of your turn, if you dealt damage, gain Guarded 1.
- Complete huge: Your first attack against a Burning target each round gains +2 damage.
- Complete card: **Ember Drake — Furnace Rush** — Deal 5 damage. Apply Burn 3. If the target already has Burn, gain Guarded 2.

## Vagabond Armour
- Source/crafting location: Wandering Pattern
- Pieces (4): Vagabond Armor Set, Hard Breastplate, Tabard, Cloth Leggings
- 2-piece minor: When equipped with no duplicate weapon names, gain +1 block on your first card each round.
- 4-piece major: Your second weapon's first attack each battle costs 1 less, minimum 0.
- Complete huge: Once per battle, swap one equipped weapon card from discard back into hand.
- Complete card: **Vagabond Armour — Improvised Pattern** — Choose one equipped weapon. Its next card this turn gains +1 damage and +1 block.
