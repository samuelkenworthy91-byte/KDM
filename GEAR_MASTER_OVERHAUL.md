## Rules

- Max equipment per survivor: **9**.
- Max weapons per survivor: **2**.
- The two equipped weapons must have different equipment names.
- Equipment card effects are universal and do not depend on fighting their source quarry/monster.
- Armour gives 2-piece, 4-piece and complete-set bonuses.
- Complete armour sets add a special ability card to the survivor deck while worn.
